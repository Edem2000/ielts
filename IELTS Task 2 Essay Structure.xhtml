Structure for all types of essay in Writing Task 2

TS=Topic Sentence (Main Idea of Paragraph)

BP=Body Paragraph

This is a simple sentence by sentence plan to write your essays and should only be used as a starting point/reference.

 

Opinion Essay

To what extent do you agree?  Do you agree or disagree?

Discussion Essay

Discuss both views and give your opinion

Advantages/Disadvantages

Discuss the advantages and disadvantages (and give your opinion)

Two Part Questions (Direct Question)

Why do you think that is? What problems does this cause?
